# Abstract

Hydrophobic eutectogels are attractive candidates for amphibious epidermal electronics because they can reduce hydration-induced swelling while maintaining ionic transport [1].
	疏水共晶凝胶是两栖表皮电子器件的有吸引力候选材料，因为其能够在维持离子传输的同时降低水合诱导的溶胀 [1]。
In this example, [[AA]] is introduced as a hydrogen-bonding monomer to strengthen the interaction between the polymer network and the eutectic solvent.
	在本示例中，[[AA]] 被引入作为氢键单体，以增强聚合物网络与共晶溶剂之间的相互作用。
The resulting interface shows stable conductivity and wet adhesion under repeated deformation.
	所得界面在反复变形下表现出稳定导电性和湿黏附性。


# Results and Discussion

The aligned fiber scaffold redistributes stress and suppresses crack propagation during cyclic loading.
	取向纤维支架能够在循环载荷过程中重新分配应力并抑制裂纹扩展。
This behavior is consistent with [[Dissipation-induced toughening]], in which local energy dissipation reduces the driving force for fracture.
	这一行为与 [[Dissipation-induced toughening]] 一致，即局部能量耗散会降低断裂驱动力。
